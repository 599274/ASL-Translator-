Reason for Errors

We only had three weeks to collect our data and train our model with 26 alphabetical classes on about 2.9k labeled data. If we had more time, we could improve our model performance significantly! The image on right is called a Confusion Matrix. It is a visual demonstration of our model performance. The X-axis is what the image actually depicts and the Y axis is what our model predicted. A perfect model would have a Confusion Matrix in which all the dark blue squares made a negative-sloped line. Our model is pretty close!
